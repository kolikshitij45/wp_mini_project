Our Mission

Reimagining the EV
Parts Economy

We believe every electric vehicle component deserves more than one life. EV ReParts is the infrastructure for a circular EV economy.

The Problem We're Solving

Electric vehicles are revolutionizing transport, but there's a hidden problem: when an EV reaches end-of-life or needs major repairs, thousands of still-functional components are discarded. Batteries, motors, and controllers that still have years of life left end up in landfills or are recycled inefficiently.

A single EV battery pack contains lithium, cobalt, nickel, and manganese — critical minerals that are expensive and energy-intensive to mine. Discarding them prematurely wastes these resources and contributes to environmental harm.

How EV ReParts Helps

Our marketplace creates a trusted secondary market for EV components. By connecting sellers with buyers, we extend the useful life of parts, reduce waste, and make EV ownership more affordable — all while building a community of sustainability-minded enthusiasts.
